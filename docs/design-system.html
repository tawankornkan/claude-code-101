Motion

Three durations, two easing curves. Enter is snappy and decelerating. Exit is brisk and accelerating. Fast (120ms) for micro-interactions, base (240ms) for transitions, slow (480ms) for page-level.

Do & Don't

The rules that define the system's identity. Violating these breaks the immersive dark-theater feel.

Do

  • Use near-black backgrounds (#121212–#1F1F1F)
  • Apply brand green only for CTAs and active states
  • Pill shape (999px) for all buttons
  • Uppercase + wide tracking on button labels
  • Keep typography compact (11–56px range)
  • Use heavy shadows (0.3–0.6 opacity) for modals
  • Express hover with bg lightness, not borders
  • Use tabular-nums on all metric/rank numerals

Don't

  • Don't use green decoratively or as a background fill
  • Don't use light surfaces as primary backgrounds
  • Don't use square buttons — pill/circle only
  • Don't use thin shadows — invisible on dark
  • Don't add new brand colors — green + grays is complete
  • Don't use relaxed line-heights on body text
  • Don't show raw gray borders — use lightness or shadow
  • Don't mix coach and member surfaces in one route
